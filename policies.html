Booking & Cancellation

  • No cancellation is permitted within 15 days of the event date.
  • Cancellations made before the 15-day window are eligible for a 90% refund; 10% is retained as an administrative and reservation fee.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days of approval.
  • Tickets are non-transferable and cannot be resold, gifted, or reassigned to another participant.
  • In the rare event of a program cancellation by The Kindling Labs, a full refund or credit toward a future session will be offered.

Privacy Policy

  • We collect only the information necessary for registration: name, contact details, and payment confirmation.
  • Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Health information shared voluntarily during sessions is kept confidential and used solely to guide safe practice.
  • Photos or videos taken during events may be used for promotional purposes unless you opt out in writing.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Participants with pre-existing medical conditions, pregnancy, cardiac concerns, respiratory disorders, or any condition that may be affected by breathing exercises must consult a qualified physician before enrolling, and should inform instructors prior to the session.

Participation is voluntary and at your own discretion; The Kindling Labs and its instructors are not liable for any injury, discomfort, or adverse reaction arising from practice.
